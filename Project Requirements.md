# Krishi Era AI
### Intelligent Agricultural Ecosystem Platform

---

## Executive Summary

Krishi Era AI is a smart platform that connects all participants in the agricultural ecosystem — farmers, buyers, transporters, and storage providers — to enable more profitable and efficient operations. As a neutral intelligence layer, we optimize coordination without owning physical assets.

---

## Target Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Value Proposition |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Farmers | Better prices, reduced waste, data-driven decisions |
| Buyers | Reliable supply, quality produce, transparent pricing |
| Transporters | Optimized routes, increased utilization, coordinated schedules |
| Storage Providers | Higher occupancy, demand-matched allocation |

---

## Core Capabilities

### Farmer Services
- Weather-based planting and harvesting recommendations
- Real market data for fair price realization
- Coordinated logistics between harvest and storage
- Market condition alerts for optimal selling windows

### Buyer Services
- Automated matching to quality produce requirements
- Data-backed fair market pricing
- Coordinated supply chain fulfillment

### Transporter Services
- Multi-shipment route optimization
- Coordinated scheduling to reduce empty trips
- Load consolidation across the network

### Storage Provider Services
- Demand-matched facility utilization
- Priority allocation based on perishability
- Reduced spoilage through intelligent timing
